FAQ

Fair questions

Why are you cheaper than an agency?

No office, no sales team, no project managers — you pay for engineering only. I'm based in Yerevan (UTC+4) with full EU overlap and US-morning availability, which keeps my costs low and my response times fast.

How do I know you won't disappear like the last freelancer?

Three things: you pay on delivery (not upfront), every job ships with documentation and a runbook your team can use without me, and my GitHub history — 130+ projects over years — is public. Disappearing acts don't leave that kind of paper trail.

Do you work with our existing tools?

Yes. n8n, Make, Zapier, plus raw Python where connectors don't exist. I deliver inside your stack so your team can maintain it — or I stay on a retainer and maintain it for you.

What about NDAs and client confidentiality?

NDA is standard for white-label work — your client never learns I exist. For direct clients: your data stays in your systems; I work through accesses you control and you revoke them when we're done.

What can't you do?

I don't take projects requiring HIPAA-regulated patient-data processing, on-site presence, or 24/7 phone support. If your project needs a 10-person team, I'll tell you in the diagnosis instead of pretending.

How do payments work?

Fixed quote → delivery → payment via Payoneer or bank transfer (USD/EUR). Retainers are billed monthly. No deposits for first-time fixes under $800.
